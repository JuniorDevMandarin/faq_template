About Minicoders

Why should kids learn to program?
Coding is one of the easiest ways for students to enhance their problem-solving skills, as well as develop the ability to bounce back after failure.
Can kids learn to code while playing?
Of course, do you know the Montessori System? the famous play-based teaching technique that uses play as a learning vehicle. Similarly, we use the Digital Metaverse environment to teach students the basics of coding and programming while having fun with an adventure game inside Minicoders.
What ages are the minicourses available for?
Our experiences are designed to cover elementary school levels (from 6 to 12 years old).
How difficult can minicourses be?
All of our content is adapted to elementary school levels following CSTA's standards. Each minicourse indicates the appropriate age to complete the course. In addition, our minicourse contains introductory videos to the experiences and concepts to help the student successfully complete the challenges.
Is Minicoders available in other languages?
Yes, you can access Minicoders in English, Spanish, Portuguese and Italian. We are currently working in Catalan, Basque and Arabic.
Does Minicoders enable communication and collaboration between students?
Yes, our experiences are based on the metaverse, so that students can communicate and collaborate with each other within the same class.
What devices are needed to conduct the minicourses?
Minicourses can be taught on Windows, Android Tablet, iPad and Chromebook devices.
How can I evaluate class results?
Minicoders offers an automated, real-time, 100% anonymous assessment and tracking portal. During the class, while the students are performing the exercises, the teacher can access a results dashboard and check the progress of each student.

Minicoders in an educational platform for kids, including Apps with video tutorials, virtual assistance and private Roblox game experiences where kids learn coding skills while playing.
